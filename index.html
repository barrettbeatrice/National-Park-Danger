Parks locations and their Death Tolls(2007-2023)

Top 5 National Parks and Their Causes of Death

Across the five parks, on average, the top three causues of death from most to least are drowning, motor vehicles, and undetermined. The causes of death that were least likely to occur and often only had one instance per park include homicide, vessel incident, and avalanche.

Most Dangerous Parks

Across the five parks, per capita of yearly visitors, North Cascades National Park has the most deaths of all parks. Of the top five, Yosemite and Yellowstone of the most variable trends. Data quality also really varied around the year 2020, likely due to the pandemic.
